About Efemena Elvis

Efemena Elvis is a freelance software developer and a graphics designer. He co-founded devCone, an open office tech development and consulting company. He currently works remotely for True Living, where he handles products graphic designs. On part time basis, he works at Anchorsoft Academy, a tech training institute, here, he instructs web development programs to students. Previously, he worked at County Choice Farms as an in-house web developer, designing and upgrading the company's website and also a management automation application.

He portrays a deep passion for software technologies, which he has built over some years of experience, consistent study and relationship with other tech enthusiast. He hopes to one day develop a deep rooted community of tech engineers and developers alike, where information and ideas will sprout out just within the twinkle of an eye.

Outside programming and design, Efemena Elvis likes to hang out with friends and family, see a movie or hit up a bookstore. Before I forget he also enjoys some cracking up recreational game of fifa and an outdoor table tennis challenge. Beep me to know more... 😉!
